Your resume for a AI Energy Economics position should clearly demonstrate your ability to work alongside AI systems while providing distinctly human value. Here's what matters most:

**Show your judgment.** Employers want to see that you can make decisions AI cannot. Include examples where your expertise, ethics, or creativity determined the outcome.

**Build relationships with your local grid operators and energy suppliers before you need them — the businesses that navigate AI energy costs best are those with personal contacts who can flag pricing opportunities and infrastructure changes early.**

**List relevant tools.** Include the AI tools you've worked with: Red-Teaming Models, Latency Profiling, Context Window Management, Recursive Feedback. Be specific about your proficiency level and the context in which you used them.
