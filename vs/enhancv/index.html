Frequently Asked Questions
Is ResumeLink better than Enhancv?
ResumeLink is better for instant sharing, ATS scoring, and free access. Enhancv is better for creative professionals who want a visually unique resume with personal branding elements.
Does Enhancv check ATS compatibility?
No. Enhancv does not provide ATS scoring. Many Enhancv visual templates are actually poor ATS performers due to complex layouts. ResumeLink AI-calculates your ATS score.
How much does Enhancv cost?
Enhancv's paid plan starts at around $24.99/month — significantly more expensive than alternatives. ResumeLink is completely free.
Is Enhancv good for corporate jobs?
Enhancv's visual style works better for creative fields. For corporate or tech roles, a clean ATS-friendly format like ResumeLink generates is more effective.
