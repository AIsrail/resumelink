Privacy Policy & Terms

Effective Date: March 5, 2026

Welcome to ResumeLink. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This document outlines how we handle your data and the terms of using our service.

1. Privacy & Data Processing

ResumeLink is built with a Privacy-First approach. We do not want your data; we only want to help you present it better.

  • Client-Side Processing: Most data extraction happens directly in your browser. We do not store your uploaded PDF files on our servers.
  • No Permanent Storage: We do not maintain a database of your personal contact information. Your profile exists only as long as your unique link is active.
  • AI Processing: We use Google Gemini AI to parse resume text. This data is processed securely and is not used by us to train models or sold to third parties.

2. How "Live Links" Work

When you generate a link for your resume, a unique, non-guessable URL is created. Important: Anyone with access to this link can view your profile. You are responsible for where and with whom you share this URL.
