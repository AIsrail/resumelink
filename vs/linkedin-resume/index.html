Frequently Asked Questions
Is ResumeLink better than LinkedIn Resume Builder?
They serve different purposes. ResumeLink gives you a standalone live profile with ATS score and QR code. LinkedIn Resume Builder is best when applying to jobs directly through LinkedIn. Most job seekers benefit from using both.
Does LinkedIn Resume Builder calculate ATS score?
No. LinkedIn does not provide ATS scoring for your resume. ResumeLink automatically calculates your ATS score using AI when you upload your resume.
Should I use LinkedIn or ResumeLink?
Use both. LinkedIn for networking and Easy Apply. ResumeLink for sharing your resume as a live link with QR code when applying outside LinkedIn or networking at events.
Can I import my LinkedIn profile to ResumeLink?
Not directly yet. The fastest way is to download your LinkedIn profile as PDF and upload it to ResumeLink — it processes in 10 seconds.
