Frequently Asked Questions
Is ResumeLink better than Kickresume?
ResumeLink is better for instant sharing, ATS scoring, and privacy. Kickresume is better if you want AI to write your resume content from scratch and need polished visual templates.
Does Kickresume have an ATS checker?
No. Kickresume does not offer ATS scoring. ResumeLink automatically calculates your ATS score using AI when you upload your resume.
Is Kickresume free?
Kickresume has a free tier but it's limited. Most useful features like AI writing and PDF download require a paid plan starting around $5/month.
Can I use Kickresume and ResumeLink together?
Yes. Use Kickresume to create a polished resume, then upload it to ResumeLink to get a shareable live link with ATS score and QR code.
