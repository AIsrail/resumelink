Frequently Asked Questions
Is ResumeLink better than Resume.io?
ResumeLink is better for speed, privacy, and zero-friction sharing with an AI-calculated ATS score. Resume.io is better for visual template variety and manual editing. Most job seekers in 2026 benefit more from a live link than a polished PDF template.
Is ResumeLink completely free?
Yes. ResumeLink is free with no signup required. You upload your resume and get a live interactive profile, ATS score, personal link, and QR code instantly. No credit card needed.
Does Resume.io charge for PDF download?
Yes. Resume.io requires a paid subscription to download your resume as a PDF. Plans start around $2.95/week. The free tier lets you build but not download.
Which is better for ATS optimization?
ResumeLink automatically calculates your ATS score using AI and shows it on your profile. This tells you exactly how your resume performs against automated screening systems. Resume.io does not offer ATS scoring.
Can I use both ResumeLink and Resume.io?
Yes. Many job seekers use Resume.io to create a polished PDF and then upload it to ResumeLink to get a shareable live link with ATS score. The tools complement each other well.
